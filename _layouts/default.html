I am an Adjunct Lecturer at the dept of Computer Science and Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). I recently graduated from the same department with a CGPA of 3.91 and recognition in the Dean’s List. My research interests are primarily in Systems, Large Language Models, Software Engineering & Security. Previously I have worked on Social Computing and Agentic AI. My recent work, CompassLLM, incorporates agentic approaches in Geo-Spatial domain.

My industrial affiliation go so far as being employed as a Software Engineer at Zelf AI, where I build data pipelines that handle millions of public social media contents daily. I craft Automation Solutions for hundreds of businesses, develop Information Retrieval, Social Media Analytics tools and collaborate with retail partners and cross-functional teams on key features.

I also contribute in interdisciplinary works. One of the recent work with IWFM, BUET where I led the Software Team, got published in Frontiers. I try to give back to the community by mentoring people in their academic and professional journeys. If you want to talk, just shoot me an email !
